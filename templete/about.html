Jan Kalyan Society

The Society was established in the year 2022 and has successfully completed 6 months of service to its members. The main object of the Society is to promote the Savings habit of the members and to grant loans to them for useful purposes with an ultimate aim of improving the living standard of the members. The Society continues to progress and offers to meet the expectations of the members, true to the Cooperative tradition and spirit.
